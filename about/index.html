About

I’m Daniel aka gnomengineer. I live in Switzerland and work as a professional C++/Qt software engineer.

Linux - OpenSource - Swiss Quality

My nickname gnomengineer is a blend (a portmanteau) of the words gnome from the high fantasy and engineer as in software engineer. I describe myself similar to a gnome regarding the facts I always have had ideas for inventions and I’m very cheerful in most aspects.

Brilliant inventors with an irrepressibly cheerful disposition - worldofwarcraft

So whenever I tinker around with my computer setup or sit down and write code, I strive for swiss quality and an organized workflow.
